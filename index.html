Meng-Yu Jennifer Kuo, PhD

 

Hello! I received my PhD degree from the Computer Vision Lab at Kyoto University, Japan, under the supervision of Prof. Shohei Nobuhara and Prof. Ko Nishino. I was a Postdoctoral Associate at University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, working with Prof. Hyun-Soo Park.

Research Interests: computer vision, 3D sensing(in water), computational photography, physics-based vision, and structure-from-motion.
