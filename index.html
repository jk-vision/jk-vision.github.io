Meng-Yu Jennifer Kuo, PhD

 

Hello! I am an Assistant Professor at Nara Women's University, Nara, Japan. I received my PhD degree (supervised by Prof. Shohei Nobuhara and Prof. Ko Nishino) from the Computer Vision Lab at Kyoto University, Japan. I was a postdoc (working with Prof. Hyun-Soo Park) at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities.

Research Interests: Computer Vision, 3D Sensing(in water), Computational Photography, Physics-Based Vision, and Structure-from-Motion.
