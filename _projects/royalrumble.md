An outdoor game event with teams of six assuming roles including King, Queen, Rook, Bishop, Knight, and Pawn. Players eliminated opponents by tearing their character cards attached to their backs, following the rule that only lower-ranked characters could be eliminated. Notably, only the Pawn had the authority to eliminate the King; once the King was eliminated, the entire team faced elimination.

At the game's outset, we briefed participants on the storyline. Alongside eliminating opponents, we integrated multiple puzzle-solving checkpoints throughout the outdoor site. Each team received a map marking the checkpoint locations. Completing puzzles earned clues, aiding participants in uncovering the truth by the game's end.

We designed diverse scoring systems for each mission, and the team achieving the highest score was declared the winner.
