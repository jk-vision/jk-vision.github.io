A one-day workshop featuring Japanese Ikebana (華道). We invited an Ikebana teacher to instruct students in the art of beautifully arranging cut stems, leaves, and flowers in containers, a cherished tradition in Japan. Participants had the opportunity to create their own Ikebana arrangements by the end of the session.
